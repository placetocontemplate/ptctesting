The Podcast

Place to Contemplate is a nascent podcast. I started it during the pandemic to tease out thoughts. By the third episode, it became a variety show with different segments (comedic, musical, etc).

The podcast is available on the podcast site or on:

I like to write poems. Painting scenes with words helps me understand.

I make booklets of my poetry. The hand-stapled ‘chapbooks’ are a dive into print design, and a way to engage with the community.

My most recent publication is ‘Floating Text’, a long-form poem about creativity.
